JobTracker v0.2

JobTracker is a ease-of-life solution for those seeking employment opportunities. Instead of manually searching through multiple employment sites for multiple job titles, JobTracker helps optimize this process by conglomerating the listings from those sites and those search queries based on your commute distance.
As of v0.2, JobTracker is configured to retrieve listings from the following sites:
  • Indeed

    • From here, you can fetch listings for multiple job titles and adjust your personal listing database based on your certain preferences.
